# User Guide

Sign in with the email and password from your invitation. What you see in the
sidebar depends on your role — if a page is missing, your role does not include it.

## Roles at a glance

| Role | What you can do |
|---|---|
| **Super Admin** | Everything — users, roles, hardware, settings, audit logs. Only one exists. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to every operational module: dashboards, cameras, live monitoring, visitors, devices, biometrics, access logs, alerts, AI Security Bot, AI Analytics, reports, notifications. No create/edit/delete/export. |

## Daily use

- **Dashboard** — headline stats, equipment issues, weekly access chart.
- **Live Monitoring** — every camera as a live tile: status, FPS, REC indicator,
  timestamp and AI detection overlays (unknown face, person, motion, intrusion).
  Filter by building/floor/camera; click ⛶ on a tile for full screen. Statuses
  refresh automatically every 10 seconds.
- **Alerts** — the security alert center: filter, acknowledge, assign, resolve
  (managing roles only). Configure your notification preferences here (email,
  critical-only, etc.).
- **AI Security Bot** — the bot analyzes every event, scores its risk (Low → Critical),
  explains why, and recommends an action. Use *AI Alerts* to review findings,
  *Alert History* to search the archive, and (admins) the *Chat Assistant* to ask
  questions like "Show today's critical alerts" or "Which cameras are offline?".
- **AI Analytics** — trends, peak hours, risk zones, health statistics, the security
  score, a 7-day risk forecast and automatic insights with recommendations.
- **Notifications** — the bell shows the latest; the Notifications page keeps the
  full archive with read/unread state.
- **Visitors** — register visits, check in/out (authorized roles), print badges
  and passes.
- **Profile** — update your name, phone, avatar and password.
- **Help & Support** — quick reference plus support contacts.

## Tips

- The top-bar search box searches whatever list page you are on.
- Tables scroll horizontally on small screens; the layout is fully responsive.
- The theme (dark/light/system) follows the appearance settings an admin sets.
